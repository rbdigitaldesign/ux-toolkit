UX review checklist

Use this interactive form to systematically assess and enhance your Canvas course across five key dimensions — clear, contextual, interactive, challenging and personalised.

about this tool

This UX review checklist combines practical usability guidelines with the University of Adelaide’s course-quality framework, aligned to the Quality Matters standards, the university’s learning and teaching strategy and contemporary learning design. It defines a failure point (0) and a baseline level (1) to ensure essential requirements are met, while levels 2 and 3 highlight opportunities for further enhancement and extension.

Using five core descriptors—clear, contextual, interactive, challenging and personalised—alongside UX best practices such as consistent navigation, meaningful labelling and timely feedback, the tool helps teams evaluate the student experience and pinpoint where targeted improvements will have the greatest impact. A balanced course will often feature scores across the 1–2–3 spectrum, reflecting both foundational compliance and higher-level innovation.

how to use this tool

  • enter your name, position and course details in the top fields
  • for each descriptor, tick any relevant checklist items and move the slider to show overall practice
  • add key strengths and areas for development in the provided text boxes
  • click generate report to view your summary table, chart and detailed output
  • use the copy, PDF or CSV buttons to export and share your findings
