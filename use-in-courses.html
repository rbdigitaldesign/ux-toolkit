UX review checklist

Use this interactive form to systematically assess and enhance your Canvas course across five key dimensions — Clear, Contextual, Interactive, Challenging and Personalised.

The five descriptors of the experience—Clear, Contextual, Interactive, Challenging and Personalised—provide a framework for aligning the Quality Matters Framework, the University of Adelaide’s learning and teaching strategy and contemporary learning design. The tool provides a failure point (0) and a baseline level (1) for minimum requirements. Levels 2 and 3 offer direction for improvement beyond baseline. This helps define the scope of the student’s learning experience and highlights where targeted enhancements may be most impactful. Balanced courses often include scores from across the 1–2–3 spectrum. Remember, this is a blend with a UX checklist too.
