UX tools for higher education

Test, measure and improve usability in your Canvas courses using these tools and resources.

Jotform

Create surveys, feedback forms, and consent forms with ease. Great for quick student testing and integrated reporting.

Visit Jotform

Optimal Workshop

Run card sorts, tree tests, and first-click tasks to validate navigation structures. Clean analytics, great for IA decisions.

Hotjar

Capture heatmaps and user recordings to see where students scroll, click, or drop off in Canvas or learning resources.

Visit Hotjar

Typeform

Create conversational surveys and quick polls. Ideal for collecting feedback mid-course in a mobile-friendly way.

Google Forms

Simple and fast for gathering student responses. Auto-links to Sheets for quick analysis. Works great in Google Workspace environments.
