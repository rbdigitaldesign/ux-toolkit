About these personas

This embedded deck was created by Kelli Knuth to support staff in designing learning experiences with empathy, accessibility, and inclusion in mind.

The personas represent diverse students across life stages, learning needs, and lived experiences. They reflect real challenges students face in navigating university systems and engaging with digital learning environments.

Use these personas to frame your UX tests, accessibility reviews, or design decisions in ways that reflect authentic student journeys.
