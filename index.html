UX toolkit for higher education

Usability testing

Moderated or unmoderated sessions to assess how users interact with your site. Tools: Maze, Lookback, Zoom.

Surveys and questionnaires

Create student-friendly feedback forms with Jotform, Google Forms or Typeform. Use short, focused questions.

Card sorting

Use Optimal Workshop to understand how users expect content to be grouped and labelled.

Heatmaps & analytics

Visualise interaction patterns with Hotjar or Microsoft Clarity. Identify pain points and content blind spots.

Testing tips

Define clear objectives, pilot your tests, and iterate based on results. Use NN/g guidelines for best practice.
